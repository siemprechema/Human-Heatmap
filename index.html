THERMODYNAMIC ANALYSIS

This engine simulates infrared radiation detection based on physiological parameters.

Note: IR cameras measure surface skin temperature, not internal organs. Colors range from Blue (Cold) to Red/White (Hot).


  • Sweat: Absorbs energy upon evaporation, lowering skin temperature.
  • Humidity: High humidity prevents sweat evaporation, nullifying the cooling effect.
  • Muscle Activity: Increases ATP consumption and blood flow, generating local heat spikes.
